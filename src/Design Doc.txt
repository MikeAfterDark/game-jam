Pocket Traits:
- Color: Red, Black, Both, Neither (start at alternating red, black and 1 void)
- Number: 0-100 (start at 0-n)
- Effect: 
    Basic,      initial state
    Void,       consumes any ball that stops on it, grows per-consumption
    Jackpot     All balls this round are counted as player's balls, all pockets count as one pocket for money

Pocket Upgrades:
    Change Color (Once per pocket)
    Add to number (rolls another smaller wheel)
    Merge Left
    Merge Right
        Merging rules: 
            - Color is set to the pocket being upgraded
            - change color tracked
            - new_num = larger - smaller

Shop:
Common:     Buy Mah Balls
Uncommmon:  Buy Mah Unusual Balls
Rare:       Buy Mah Shiny Balls
Legendary:  Buy Mah Steel Balls
Unique:     Buy Mah Divine Balls


Ball Ideas:
- Type: Rarity (for shop/cost)
- Size, momentum, and base-friction
- Deals # Damage, Grants # Armour or Heals for #, rarety increases #

Balls:
- Damage Stone - deals damage
- Armour Stone - gives armour
- Heal Stone - gets consumed, heals (max hp cap)
- Number Ball: +X to number it lands on
- Snowball (Armour): leaves an icy trail behind it for 0.2ms
- Slimeball (Poison DoT): leaves a slime trail behind it for 0.1ms
- Splitter Ball (Damage): splits after X collisions, each half is 50% effective
- Fireball (Damage): triggers the pocket twice by exploding
- Unstoppa-Ball (2x Damage): cant be knocked aside, phases through other Unstoppa-Balls

Money/Placing Bets:
    Player gets to place their bet first, and alternate with the enemy, who will
    put bets on the most likely outcomes. "Winning" bets bonus payout money, otherwise
    money = ball rarity * X * (1+num balls that were already counted in that pocket)

Bets:
    Even
    Odd
    Red
    Black

Full Game Loop:
- Player starts with 2 balls, 3 slots, 9 max slots, can buy slots anytime outside of spinning, costs exponential
- Spinning sends the balls, physics and collisions happen
    - Once all balls stop, process one ball at a time (speed option in settings)
    - Start with jackpot, then go per pocket in direction of spin until get to a pocket with balls
    - Balls from pocket selected randomly to trigger
    - consumptions delete the ball without triggering
    - bets apply bonuses to ball trigger if pocket is right
    - money and damage are summed up as the scoring progresses, adding to the totals at the end
    - at the end, reset the balls to pre-spin except for consumptions, board to pre-jackpot
- After a spin, user can:
    - sell a ball (hovering over their list), or buying a ball slot    
    - buy a ball by 'rolling' the ball shop via opening the drawer
        - each 'roll' is given some rarity, and the balls generated are based off that
        - rerolls by left-right motion on the handle (or closing then opening)
        - bought balls are added to the end of the queue
    - Set/Change their bet
    - FORCED AFTER EACH SPIN: Modify the table, per pocket, with the options to:
        - Merge with the pocket to the left or right, new_num = larger - smaller, colour stays the same
        - Change colour 
        - Add to the number
[REPEAT]
- If player dies then show end screen
- If enemy dies then give the player money bonus and send out the next enemy
    - after all the enemies die, the player wins the game
